ment features

ment is built to give you maximum control and privacy when it comes to ai chat. here are some highlights:

local large language model (lllm)

no external servers. no data leaks. everything runs directly on your apple device, giving you complete privacy and autonomy.

customization & tweakability

  • system prompts: define your ai’s role, style, and constraints
  • branching conversations: follow different paths of a converation by branching responses with different topics you'd love to delve in to
  • assistant profiles: swap personalities or knowledge sets easily

offline usage

never worry about a lost internet connection.
ment doesn’t need the cloud to function--perfect for travel, remote areas, or simply preserving bandwidth

pdf integration [in development]

soon, you’ll be able to feed pdf documents directly into ment to reference during your chats, unlocking a new level of contextual conversation and assistant profiles

more features are on the way as we continue to build ment!
