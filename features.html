Ment Features

Ment is built to give you maximum control and privacy when it comes to AI chat. Below are some highlights:

Local Large Language Model

No external servers. No data leaks. Everything runs directly on your Apple device, giving you complete privacy and autonomy.

Customization & Tweakability

  • System Prompts: Define your AI’s role, style, and constraints.
  • Branching Conversations: Keep multiple threads of discussion open with different contexts.
  • Assistant Profiles: Swap personalities or knowledge sets easily.

Offline Usage

Never worry about a lost internet connection. Ment doesn’t need the cloud to function—perfect for travel, remote areas, or simply preserving bandwidth.

PDF Integration (In Development)

Soon, you’ll be able to feed PDF documents directly into Ment to reference during your chats, unlocking a new level of contextual conversation.

More features are on the way as we continue to build Ment!
