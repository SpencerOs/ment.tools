ment

empowering your ai experience with privacy and control

Discover the unparalleled advantages of ment, designed to provide you with maximum control and privacy in AI interactions:

local large language model (lllm)

experience true autonomy with ment’s on-device processing. by running entirely on your apple device, ment ensures:

  • complete privacy: your data remains solely on your device, eliminating concerns about external server vulnerabilities
  • enhanced security: local execution minimizes exposure to potential data breaches, offering a secure environment for your interactions
  • reduced latency: enjoy faster response times as ment processes requests instantly without relying on internet connectivity

customization & flexibility

tailor your ai experience to suit your preferences:

  • system prompts: define your ai’s role, tone, and constraints to align with your specific needs
  • branching conversations: explore diverse topics by branching responses, allowing for in-depth discussions on subjects of interest
  • assistant profiles: easily switch between different ai personalities or knowledge bases to match various contexts

offline functionality

stay connected, even when offline

  • uninterrupted access: ment operates seamlessly without internet access, ideal for travel, remote locations, or conserving bandwidth
  • consistent performance: maintain full functionality regardless of connectivity, ensuring reliability wherever you are

upcoming feature: pdf integration

We are excited to announce an upcoming enhancement:

  • document referencing: soon, you’ll be able to input pdf documents directly into ment, enabling the ai to reference and incorporate document content into your conversations

at ment, we are committed to continuous improvement and innovation. stay tuned for more features designed to enhance your local ai experience.
